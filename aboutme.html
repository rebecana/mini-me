About me

Hi, I'm Rebeca. I'm studying Web Design in Bucharest. I like to call myself a simple person. Even my design of this personal website is simple. "mini me" is a name which I choosed because despite of all "adult stuff", I still wanna keep my childish side alive. Surely, it can be a reference to "minimalism", too. It's just the perfect name. My background image is actually a kind of logo I wanted to design, in Illustrator. It was supposed to be a "logo" to use as a profile picture to all my social accounts. I couldn't find a photo to be exacly what I wanted, so I said to myself: Why can't you make one? So I did.

I like The concept of minimalism is one I embrace. Not only in design. There are other ares of my life in which I decide to keep the less most important things despite the rest. I rather focus on things that really matters that to have too much and feel overwhelmed. I actually think that living this king of meaningful life leads more to happiness than having too much of things we thought we need them to be happy. Then it turns out it wasn't actually like that. Comin back to me, I like to keep my mystery in my works, still to focus on what matters. Sounds like a paradox but it's my personal touch. Surely, not all the time.. The point is balance.
